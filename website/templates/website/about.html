About Us

Braintree Educational Resources offers comprehensive educational services for private further/higher education colleges, training providers, and other independent educational based organizations and centres in Nigeria and abroad. Braintree Educational Resources also provides education consultancy services to students who desiring to study abroad through our partnerships with institutions in kenya, the UK, Canada, Sweden, Germany, Hungary, Denmark, Cyprus, Australia and New Zealand. We have established expertise in academic and administrative management, educational-based training, inclusion and inclusive practices as well as strategic management and planning of private education and training providers. The founder of BER, Yusuf Mohammed, a former lecturer with over 30 years experience in the academia in such roles as as Lecturer, Course Coordinator, Exam committee scertary, seminar and workshop committee secretary, accreditation committee member backed with courses in education management, strategic management & leadership, improvement planning, student recruitment and selection and quality management has brpught these years of experienve into evolving ber as a consultancy support to private further/higher education colleges, training providers, and other independent educational based organizations and centres in the UK and abroad for their growth and success as an institution. Braintree Educational Resources consists of a dedicated team of experienced professionals with many combined years of experience within education, training, consulting and management. Our History Braintree Educational Resources was established in 2007 in Jos, Nigeria. Our experienced education counsellors are ready to advise students on courses and career opportunities according to their educational background, interests, aptitude, and aspirations. Have a broad network with education providers, we are able to suggest a wide range of study options and programs to meet the students' needs. Our firm is proficient in advising potential Students or Migrants regarding any Australian Visa Matters from Student Visas to Skilled Visas, Business Visas, and Family Visas etc. Please do fill in Our Enquiry Form available in this website, if you are interested in finding out more information regarding Australian Visas.
