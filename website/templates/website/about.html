About Us

Braintree Educational Resources offers comprehensive educational services for private schools, training providers, and other independent educational based organizations and centres in Nigeria and abroad. We also provide education consultancy services to students desiring to study abroad through our partnerships with institutions 33 countries.

We have established expertise in academic and administrative management, educational-based training, inclusion and inclusive practices as well as strategic management and planning of private education and training providers.

Founded 2007 in Jos, Nigeria and managed by a team of experienced professionals with many combined years of experience within education, training, consulting and management Braintree provides training in different areas of education and also works with school owners in terms of school management and growth. With a network of education providers in over thirty-three countries, our team provides counselling to students on courses and career opportunities according to their educational background, interests, aptitude, and aspirations.
